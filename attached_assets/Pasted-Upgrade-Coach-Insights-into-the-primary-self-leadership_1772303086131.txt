C) Grace 2.0: compassionate accountability (not a loophole)

Grace must:

help you recover without shame

but still hold you accountable structurally

Implement:

Grace Bank per week (configurable)

Ex: 2 grace credits/week

Used grace prevents a rhythm penalty

Grace requires a “recommit”
When applying grace, prompt:

“What’s the smallest recommitment you can make?”
Options:

Do Minimum Version now

Reschedule for tomorrow

Swap with a lighter task
This turns grace into action, not avoidance.

Grace patterns become insights
Coach shows:

“Grace used most often on Tue evenings → consider lighter scheduling or Minimum Version”

If grace frequently used on same habit → recommend redesign (cue/friction).

Grace for tasks too
Allow “Grace this task”:

Doesn’t delete it

Moves it to a “Recovery lane” with a suggested re-plan option

Avoids overwhelming backlog
